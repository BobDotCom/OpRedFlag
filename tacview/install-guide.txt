before starting Tacview:




Copy flightgear-oprf.xml into C:\ProgramData\Tacview\Data\Database\Default Properties




To install better terrain:
==========================
Go to http://viewfinderpanoramas.org/Coverage%20map%20viewfinderpanoramas_org3.htm

Download the areas you want.

Unzip the hgt files into C:\ProgramData\Tacview\Data\Terrain\SRTM3



To get photo textures at LIPA and North Norway:
===============================================

Copy all files in 'textures' into C:\ProgramData\Tacview\Data\Terrain\Textures
